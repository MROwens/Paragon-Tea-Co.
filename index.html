Our Teas

Green

Hand-flattened jade-green leaves create a mellow-sweet taste with chestnut overtones. Natural sweetness from the jade-green leaf establishes complexity in this renowned sweet nutty tea.

Black

Grown in the mineral-rich soil of the mountainous highlands, shrouded by ethereal mist. The buds and leaves are plucked by hand and processed into tight coils with golden tips. With this tea you can expect a subtle floral and light honey taste with a lingering sweet caramel finish.

Oolong

Young, tender buds and leaves are gently hand-plucked, withered and oxidized, resulting in a distinctive multicolored appearance. A medium-bodied tea with sweet caramel notes and a subtle plum and apricot finish.

Herbal

The enticing flavors of these fruit-laden delights are steeped with a delicious medley of dragonfruit, orange, mango, guava, or luscious strawberry. It's a tropical journey, soothed with the lush tropical fragrance of rose and orange flower blossoms.
