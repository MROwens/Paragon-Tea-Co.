OUR STORY

Paragon Tea Company began in 1998 by health enthusiasts Cindy and Rob Owens. While traveling throughout India and Indonesia on a spiritual holiday in 1994, Rob discovered his love for the organic, raw power of hand-picked teas. Back in America, he and Cindy began experimenting with their own blends specifically designed to treat certain ailments. These holistic teas gained popularity with friends and loved ones, leading to the birth of Paragon.

WHAT WE'RE ABOUT

Today, Paragon Tea Company strives to provide the same quality, purity, and benefits that Rob experienced two decades ago. We import only the finest leaves and herbs to give our customers the most authentic, rich taste. All of our ingredients are organic, natural, and 100% fair-trade.
